Flow explained

Operational path used by Quick Check.
1. Select market
Pick one or more affected markets. Each selected market opens in the aligned column below.
2. Monitor independently
Each market keeps its own timer, engineer, and action buttons without replacing the others.
3. Reach OPS or on-call
Use the market actions to open OPS guidance or the call script for that specific market.
4. Stop when resolved
Stopping one market closes only that column and preserves the rest on screen.
